Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you fill out a contact form, enroll in a course, or interact with our AI assistant. This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Class / Grade
  • Parent / Guardian name
  • City
  • Any messages or queries you submit

We also automatically collect certain technical data when you use our site, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This is used for analytics and to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide academic counselling
  • Process course enrollments and confirm your seat
  • Improve our platform and personalise your learning experience
  • Send you important updates, promotional offers, and educational content (only with your consent)
  • Monitor usage patterns to enhance site performance and security

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering services (e.g., hosting, analytics) – they are bound by confidentiality agreements
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, where your information may be transferred as a business asset

4. Data Security

We implement industry‑standard security measures to protect your personal data, including encryption, firewalls, and secure server configurations. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features.

6. Third‑Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites (e.g., WhatsApp, Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third‑party sites. We encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal data.

7. Children’s Privacy

We provide educational services to students of all ages. For children under 13, we require parental consent before collecting any personal information. Parents/guardians may contact us to review or request deletion of their child's data.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Object to processing of your data in certain situations

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sankalpdigitalpathshala.online.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@sankalpdigitalpathshala.online
  • WhatsApp: Chat Now
  • Through our Contact Form
